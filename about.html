About me?

Hi! My name is Lyra, a 20 years old male currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science. With a strong enthusiasm for web design I am dedicated to creating visually engaging and user friendly websites. My journey has not only honed my technical skills but also deepened my understanding of user experience and interface design, enabling me to blend aesthetics with functionality effectively.

When I'm not immersed in coding, I enjoy spending my free time indulging in music and gaming. These passions fuel my creativity and provide a much needed balance to my academic pursuits. Additionally, I cherish the moments I spend with my girlfriend, who continually inspires me and supports my endeavors.

I am particularly interested in exploring the intersection of technology and design, where I aim to develop websites that not only capture attention but also enhance usability. As I advance in my studies, I look forward to collaborating on innovative projects that push the boundaries of web design and technology.
